My Story

Fashion Designer & Blogger

Hey!!! this is Peter Mwansa, populary know as PeterMyLife.
I the CEO and Founder of HouseOfPML and an award winning Fashion Desinger and Blogger based in Lusaka Zambia.
My interests in fashion began at a tender age but it was not until 2016 when my Entrepreneurial endevors as a fashion designer and blogger started. I draw my inspiration in design from my mother who I would describe as a very fashionable woman.

During my whole career in Fashion and Design, I have had opportunities to work with the some of Zambia's notable artists and business personnels among them is Cleo Ice Queen and the rest. Due to the increase in my client base which keeps on incresing by the day, I wish to expand my brand across the borders of Zambia and I am striving hard to accomplish hard by working hard and I providing the best services to my clients. My career has been quiet a good one I would say to the recognition that I have received from notable institutions like Diamond TV of Zambia who awarded me with an award of Desgner of the year 2021 at their award ceremony which they had. That is just one of the many honors and awards that I have received so far in my career and I am yet to receive more and even bigger.
